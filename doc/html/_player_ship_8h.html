Detailed Description

 Defines a PlayerShip
Author
Olivier Brewaeys

A playership is spawned at the center of the bottom of the screen. This is at (8, 19) for a rectangular 15x20 grid (the world dimensions). It starts with 10 hitpoints, a speed of 1. Its size is 3 width units and 2 height units.

It can only move sideways, so it remains at the bottom of the screen. The player can shoot bullets to destroy enemy ships and move to avoid being shot itself. When the playership's HP reaches zero it dies and the level ends.
